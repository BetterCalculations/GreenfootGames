Hello dear player and welcome to Car Racer 800,
With a you move to the left.
With d you dodge to the right.
You brake with s.
Use the spacebar to accelerate.
Be careful, your opponents who are ghost drivers will become faster after each round.
The game has no end unless you come against an opponent then you lose.
Importantly, the resolution and speed are not adjustable
Your highest score is saving automatic.
